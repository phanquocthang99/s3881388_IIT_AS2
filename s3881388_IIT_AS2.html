Project Idea

Overview

Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, several games have become popular with people during the quarantine. For example, Among Us, Fall Guys, and the newest game that has been discussed widely at this time-Play Together. Those games have some similar features: they are casual social-network games, a vast variety of people play them at a time, and the number of players plummeted after a period of time. Learning from their failure, I decided to develop a game that is based on the Unity platform that can draw attention to gamers and maintain a stable number of players over time.

Motivation

In 2020, there was a big change in the game industry. While people were getting bored with action-adventure games with hyper-realistic graphics, the wave of casual social-network games was a new taste for them to experience. However, these games could not keep players long enough because of the lack of evolution after the number of downloads broke the record. For example, Among Us on Steam shows that there were only 271 players maximum at a time in January 2020, and the number of players rose rapidly to 438,524 players maximum in September 2020, but there were 17,040 peak players left in the last 30 days. The reason is all about their in-game content. The developers have not changed anything much since they created this game (no new mode, no event, no competition, etc.). The players will soon realize there isnt any goal for them to achieve in this game after a while. The motivation for me to create a new game is to satisfy myself and a group of players who are expecting a rich-content social-network game. And by using Unity, I can create a game that has good design, smooth animation, and can be released on many platforms to attract players.

Description

Here are various features that must include in the game:

  1. Tutorial stage: Although this is a casual game (players can perform actions easily), players need a guide to get used to its functions to have a better experience. Thus, tutorial stages will be set up first for every new player, and they will get some rewards after finishing each stage.
  2. In-app purchase: There should be a store for players to buy extra items, additional content, or they can exchange money for the games currency. This is a free-to-play game, so the main profit that a developer can gain from this game is by selling items from the store. 
  3. Micro-phone: One of the largest obstacles in social-network games is communication, since players have to type anything they want to say. I understand that a group of players can use another platform like Discord to communicate, but I want to create a virtual world where any player can talk to others. Therefore, Im going to put microphone features in the game.
  4. User interface: My goal is to design an interface that is as simple as possible. On the start screen, there should be start, setting, and announcement buttons. Next, the in-game user interface will include buttons in the left corner of the screen to perform necessary functions, like customizing characters, settings, friend list, missions, turning on/off voice chat,... etc. Moreover, all of them will be simplified by icon in order not to take too much space on the screen.
  5. Friend system: This is a social-network casual game, and its main goal is to connect people together. Therefore, I especially focus on the friend system. Accounts that have already been added together as friends can increase their relationship points by doing tasks, and when they reach a certain point, they can open some special functions.
  6. Ranking system: What keeps players from getting bored is the competitive nature of the game. There will be an in-game chart button that displays the rank of the top players, who have achievement points above other players. Besides that, every player will be given their rank depending on their points, so they can keep their gaming process on track.
  7. Loot box system: This is a type of gambling that allows players to spend in-game money to receive a random item, and I put this system in because I understand that one of the most addictive features for players is gambling. By using the Random Number Generator (RNG) system, various types of loot boxes can be created (Open mystery boxes, draw cards,...). There is an area being created where players can test their luck to get unique items or special characters, and those can not be traded. This feature arouses the desire of players to get the item they want.
  8. Announcement: I expect that the content of this game will be updated every two weeks, and any updated features should be announced to players. Therefore, I will make an announcement page pop-up every time players open the game. There is also an option that players can choose to let this page not appear again.

9.      Feedback: Developers themselves can not make a good game without receiving any feedback from their customers, so I will create a feedback page for players to fix errors or acquire their ideas for further development.

Outcome

If this game is released successfully and draws the attention of many people, there will be a whole new era of social-network games. First of all, this game is a combination of many casual games, so it will cover up all the mistakes that the previous developer made, so it can keep the number of players stable. Cloud computing

What does it do?

There are three types of cloud computing state of the art: Software as a Service (SaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS). People can choose any type of these forms depending on their purpose. SaaS is a distribution model for software. Instead of downloading software to run locally on a personal computer, the program or application is hosted by a third-party vendor and then accessed. SaaS applications are also known as web-based software, on-demand software, and hosted software. PaaS type provides users with the platform to develop their program, software, or website, and since it does not require any special or specific environment for the application, users can work on it conveniently. The last thing is the IaaS type. This technology gives users the key to access virtual resources, including online infrastructures, servers, software,... etc. The most specific instance of cloud computing that is being used widely in the world is email. Any email platform at present applies cloud computing technology as users can store and access files on those online platforms. Another use of cloud computing technology is for education. Teachers at RMIT University are using a platform called Canvas to deliver course lectures, materials, assignments, and syllabuses to students, and either students or lecturers can participate in the discussion forum. In the future, people are expecting that they can develop games based on cloud computing technology, and that will be a new face of the game industry. In the near future (about 3 years), games based on Cloud Computing technology will be released by many game companies. Their goal of applying Cloud Computing to games is to lower the system requirements for any game that they have developed. By applying Cloud Computing, players can run a game on a created server remotely while the image of that game is displayed directly on their devices. This will expand the number of people who can join the game because they dont need to update their devices to the latest to experience it. To make it possible, companies such as Nintendo, Sony, or Microsoft need to design a cloud server for their games and introduce them widely to players all over the world. Moreover, as these Cloud Computing game types will allow players to run them on a remote server, a strong internet connection is required, and developers expect that at least the speed of 5G is enough to run them smoothly.

What is the likely impact?

The evolution of Cloud Computing will impact many industries in the world, and one potential impact that I can predict is tourism. Cloud Computing technology has made it easier for travel agencies to operate. Web-based travel search, smartphone, chatbot, data analytics, and travel ERP & CRM tools have reduced manual and time-consuming tasks for those assisting with procedures and planning for their customers. By the development of this technology, both businesses and customers are impacted the most. Firstly, it helps travel agencies lower their funds required to operate a website. The tourism industry is very competitive. Demand may spike during seasonal or holidays and also bottom out at times. When you invest in an on-premise infrastructure, you are required to spend a fair amount of money initially on storage and processing when demand is high, and then, if customer demand changes, you also have to accept that you don't really need that amount of bandwidth. Cloud computing is a pay-on-demand service. As demand grows, the number of servers and storage can easily increase. As the traffic decreases, the configuration decreases and so does the price. When sharing data or making online payments, we are extremely vulnerable to identity theft. Many consumers are concerned about this. While the internal infrastructure is already safe, IT teams are constantly looking for methods to enhance it in order to resist the current threats, and these additional services are typically costly. Cloud computing providers, on the other hand, are constantly updating their security to secure their infrastructure.
